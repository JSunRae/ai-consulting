# Jason Rae Brand Intelligence Brief

Last updated: 2026-04-24

## Canonical Positioning

- Primary identity: Senior Data Analyst EMEA | Commercial Analytics Architect | Applied AI & Decision Intelligence Leader
- Consulting positioning: Commercial analytics and applied AI advisor for DACH businesses that need practical forecasting, pricing, margin, CRM, and decision-intelligence systems
- Core brand line: Commercial operator -> analytics architect -> applied AI leader.
- Brand promise: I help European businesses move from fragmented reporting and unreliable forecasts to trusted commercial decision systems.
- Differentiator: Commercial operating experience plus hands-on implementation in Power BI, Python, SQL, machine learning, and GPT-4 systems.

## Public Story

Use this narrative consistently across website, resume, LinkedIn, and sales materials:

I help businesses fix the data, logic, and governance problems that stop leaders from making good commercial decisions. Then I build the AI and analytics systems that make those decisions repeatable.

## Messaging Priorities

- Lead with: forecasting, pricing, margin, CRM, incentives, executive reporting, decision systems
- Emphasize: practical implementation, auditable workflows, messy real-world data, commercial credibility
- Avoid leading with: generic AI consultant language, prompt engineering as a primary value prop, pure developer framing, startup-style AI hype
- Preferred phrases: decision systems, commercial analytics foundations, executive-ready outputs, deterministic AI workflows, no AI theater

## Core Offers

1. Commercial Analytics Health Check
   30-minute diagnostic plus follow-up summary. Diagnose breakdowns in reporting, forecasting, pricing, CRM, or margin visibility.
2. AI Readiness and Roadmap Sprint
   2 weeks. Prioritized AI roadmap tied to ROI, feasibility, and data readiness.
3. Commercial Analytics Foundation Fix
   3 to 6 weeks. Repair KPI logic, CRM quality, forecasting, pricing, margin, dashboard sprawl, and business-review inputs.
4. First AI Use Case Deployment
   4 to 8 weeks. Build one practical, auditable AI workflow.
5. AI Training for Commercial Teams
   Half day, full day, or multi-week enablement for sales, finance, analysts, managers, and executives.

## Best-Fit Buyers

- Geography: Germany, DACH, Europe
- Company size: 50 to 500 employees
- Titles: Managing Director, Finance Director, Commercial Director, Sales Director, Operations Manager, Head of BI or Analytics, Transformation Lead, COO
- Trigger problems: unreliable forecasts, untrusted CRM, dashboard sprawl, pricing exceptions, unexplained margin decline, overloaded analysts, political business reviews, AI interest without a landed use case
