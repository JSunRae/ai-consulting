## Emphasize

- Team leadership and delivery ownership
- Commercial judgment and stakeholder communication
- Ability to translate business problems into analytical systems
- Hands-on Python, SQL, Power BI, AI workflow, and reporting credibility
- Cross-functional trust-building and governance

## De-emphasize

- Any suggestion of proven personal book-of-business ownership
- Overstated German fluency
- Heavy Kubernetes / platform-engineering ownership claims
