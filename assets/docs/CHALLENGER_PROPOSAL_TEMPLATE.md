Last updated: 2026-05-12

## 2. Current Buyer Assumption

State what the client thinks they need.

Example:

`The apparent need is an AI copilot purchase, chatbot rollout, reporting rebuild, pricing reset, or workflow automation project.`

## 3. Commercial Reframe

State the deeper issue in one blunt but defensible paragraph.

Template:

`The deeper issue is not only tool selection or build capacity. It is weak workflow design, unclear ownership, inconsistent data quality, and a control model that would allow the wrong automation or reporting logic to scale poor decisions.`

## 5. Operating Impact

Write the day-to-day management consequence.

Template:

`This is why leadership meetings begin by debating whose number is right, why service escalations become harder instead of easier, and why AI pilots look interesting but do not become operating capability.`
