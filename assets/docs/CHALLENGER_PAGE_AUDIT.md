# Challenger Page Audit

Last updated: 2026-05-12

## Objective

Confirm that the core public pages teach before they sell, challenge weak buyer assumptions, and take control of the next step rather than drifting into generic advisory language.

## Audit Framework

Each page was reviewed against the Challenger sequence:

1. warmer
2. reframe
3. rational drowning
4. emotional impact
5. new way
6. your solution

Classification options:

- keep
- lightly patch
- rewrite
- demote from conversion path

- Keep the current architecture:
  - homepage and services drive the commercial conversation
  - blog and lead magnets teach and qualify
