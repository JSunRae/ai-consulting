# AI / Analytics Institutional Affiliation Plan for Jason Rae

## 1. Executive summary

Jason Rae's strongest, evidence-backed positioning is not "AI academic" or "policy celebrity." It is: a commercial analytics operator who became a hands-on analytics architect and practical applied AI leader, with real delivery credibility in forecasting, pricing, margin logic, CRM governance, executive reporting, workflow automation, and governed AI enablement.

The best affiliation strategy is therefore:

1. Join reputable Germany/EU or governance-adjacent institutions where ordinary membership is legitimate and not overstated.
2. Prefer affiliations that can evolve into visible contribution: working groups, chapter activity, published commentary, webinars, mentoring, or standards input.
3. Avoid grand titles unless formally granted in writing.
4. Use plain "Member" language for normal memberships.
5. Treat any "delegate", "advisor", "council", "board", or "mentor" title as unusable until officially confirmed.

The highest-fit, lowest-risk targets are:

- Gesellschaft fuer Informatik e.V. (GI) plus FBKI
- German Data Science Society (GDS) e.V.
- European Commission Apply AI Alliance
- ISACA membership with Germany Chapter
- Open Data Institute (ODI) individual membership

These align with Jason's real profile across applied AI adoption, analytics maturity, governance, and business-facing transformation. They also avoid the vanity-credential trap.

- Strong alignment with Jason's blend of informatics, applied AI, analytics, and professional practice.

### 2. German Data Science Society (GDS) e.V.

Why it fits:

- Directly matches analytics, ML, and data-science identity without requiring academic overreach.
- Low cost and Germany-based.
- Better aligned to Jason than purely academic research societies because GDS explicitly addresses applied Data Science and professional networking.

Best positioning use:

- `Member, German Data Science Society (GDS) e.V.`

### 3. Apply AI Alliance

Why it fits:

- Official European Commission initiative, not a vanity community.
- Strong match to AI adoption, policy-aware implementation, and responsible uptake.
- Fastest low-cost path to externally defensible EU AI ecosystem participation.

Best positioning use:

- Mention only as community/stakeholder participation unless Jason actually contributes content or joins consultative activity.
- Stronger for website / Featured section than for LinkedIn Experience.

### 4. ISACA + Germany Chapter

Why it fits:

- Gives Jason a more serious governance, trust, control, and digital-risk flank.
- Useful if he wants to speak credibly about responsible AI operating models, governance, controls, and adoption risk.
- Germany chapter creates local relevance.

Best positioning use:

- `Professional Member, ISACA`
- Do not imply certification or governance authority from membership alone.

### 5. Open Data Institute (ODI)

Why it fits:

- Good match for data ethics, data maturity, data governance, and practical AI trust positioning.
- Strong complement to consulting offers around readiness, operating models, and responsible adoption.

Best positioning use:

- `Individual Member, Open Data Institute`
